Meet Dan
Dan Funderburgh is an artist, illustrator, and designer. He currently works in Brooklyn, NY. His work shows a deep appreciation of historical ornament. Dan also works as an instructor in surface design at School of Visual Arts.
He designs multiple types of art:
  • Packaging
  • Public Art
  • Installations
  • Book covers
  • Wallpaper
